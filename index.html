About Us

Shabib Consultant Services, led by Sheikh Shabib Omar, provides Islamic-centered support services with a focus on marriage, family life, counseling, and religious education. We serve the Muslim community with integrity, confidentiality, and professionalism—fully aligned with Sharia and compliant with Kenyan law.
